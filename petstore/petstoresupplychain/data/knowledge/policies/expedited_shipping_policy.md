# Pet Product Expedited Shipping Authorization Policy

**Policy Number:** PET-POL-004
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2024
**Owner:** VP Pet Supply Chain Operations
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes authorization levels and procedures for expedited shipping of pet products to retail partners, ensuring continuity of supply while controlling transportation costs. Special handling requirements for live animals, perishable pet food, and temperature-sensitive health products are addressed.

## 2. Authorization Levels

### 2.1 Standard Expedited (≤ $3,000 shipping cost)
- **Authorized by:** Pet Supply Chain Manager
- **Approval method:** Email or procurement system approval
- **Turnaround:** Same business day

### 2.2 Premium Expedited ($3,001 – $15,000 shipping cost)
- **Authorized by:** Director, Pet Supply Chain Operations
- **Approval method:** Formal written approval with business justification
- **Turnaround:** Within 4 business hours

### 2.3 Emergency Freight (> $15,000 or critical retail stockout)
- **Authorized by:** VP Pet Supply Chain or Retail Operations Director
- **Approval method:** Emergency authorization form + verbal confirmation
- **Turnaround:** Immediate
- **Note:** For critical retail partner stockouts (days of supply < 3), the Retail Operations Director may authorize air freight at **up to 3x standard shipping cost**

## 3. Triggering Conditions

Expedited shipping is justified when:
- Days of supply at retail partner DCs falls below **reorder point** and standard replenishment will not arrive in time
- Retail partner promotional event commitments are at risk
- Pet food products approaching best-by date require faster rotation
- Quality rejection of incoming shipment requires emergency replacement
- Seasonal demand spike (holidays, National Pet Day, etc.) exceeds forecast

## 4. Special Handling Requirements

| Product Category | Shipping Requirement |
|---|---|
| Perishable pet food | Temperature-controlled (35-75°F); max 5 days transit |
| Liquid supplements | Upright orientation; leak-proof secondary packaging |
| Fragile items (aquariums) | White-glove freight; no stacking |
| Flea/tick medications | Climate-controlled; tamper-evident seals |

## 5. Cost Allocation

- Expedited shipping costs attributed to **root cause** cost center
- If caused by supplier late delivery: charge back to supplier account where contract permits
- Monthly reporting of expedited shipping spend to Finance and VP Pet Supply Chain
- Retail partner contractual obligations may require PetStore to absorb expedited costs

## 6. Carrier Selection

- Use contracted carriers (TailWag Logistics for Northeast) where possible
- Spot market carriers require procurement team approval
- Temperature-controlled carriers must be on the approved cold-chain carrier list
- Air freight carriers must hold live animal/perishable goods certification
