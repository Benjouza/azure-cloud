# Pet Product Alternate Supplier Qualification and Approval Policy

**Policy Number:** PET-POL-007
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Owner:** Director, Pet Product Sourcing
**Review Cycle:** Annual

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the process for qualifying and approving alternate pet product suppliers to ensure continuity of supply while maintaining product safety, quality, and regulatory compliance standards for all pet food, toys, accessories, and health products.

## 2. Approved Vendor List (AVL)

The Approved Vendor List is maintained by Pet Product Sourcing and includes:
- Primary suppliers for each pet product category
- Pre-qualified alternate suppliers with validated capability
- Emergency-qualified suppliers with conditional approval

## 3. Standard Qualification Process

**Timeline:** 45–90 business days (pet products require additional safety testing)

1. Supplier capability assessment and facility inspection
2. Pet product safety certification review (AAFCO for food, ASTM F963 for toys)
3. Sample production run and quality testing (including pet safety hazard assessment)
4. Commercial terms negotiation
5. Legal review and contract execution
6. System setup and product listing integration

## 3.1 Emergency Qualification

**Timeline:** 72 hours

For stockout emergencies, pre-qualified alternate suppliers from the AVL may be activated under these conditions:

1. **Director approval** from Pet Product Sourcing or Supply Chain Operations
2. **Quality & Safety sign-off** based on review of alternate supplier's product safety certifications and last 3 batch test records
3. **Documented business justification** including stockout impact on retail partners
4. Initial order limited to **50% of standard order quantity** until full qualification complete
5. 100% incoming safety inspection for first 3 shipments from alternate source
6. Verify pet food products have current AAFCO compliance documentation

## 4. Dual-Sourcing Authorization

When triggered by Supplier Escalation Runbook Tier 3:
- Dual-sourcing may proceed **without reducing** the minimum volume commitment to the primary supplier for **180 days**
- After 180 days, volume reallocation requires VP Pet Products approval
- Dual-sourcing cost differential absorbed by Supply Chain Operations budget

## 5. Decision Authority

| Action | Authority |
|---|---|
| Add to AVL (standard) | Director, Pet Product Sourcing |
| Emergency activation from AVL | Director, Supply Chain Operations |
| Dual-sourcing initiation | VP Pet Products (per Escalation Runbook Tier 3) |
| Primary supplier replacement | SVP Retail Operations + Legal |
