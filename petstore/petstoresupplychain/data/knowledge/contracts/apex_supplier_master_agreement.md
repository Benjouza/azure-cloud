# FurEver Toys Inc – Master Supply Agreement

**Parties:** PetStore Retail Inc. ("Buyer") and FurEver Toys Inc. ("Supplier")
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Term:** 3 years with annual renewal option

---

## 1. Scope of Supply

FurEver Toys Inc shall supply the following pet product categories:
- Interactive Puzzle Feeder Dog Toys (PRD-110)
- Catnip Mouse Variety Packs (PRD-111)
- Custom pet toys per PetStore product specifications
- Seasonal and promotional pet toy collections

## 2. Pricing and Payment

- **Payment terms:** Net 30 from date of invoice
- **Annual price review:** Permitted once per calendar year with 90-day notice
- **Volume commitment:** Minimum $250,000 annual purchase volume
- **Promotional pricing:** 15% discount for quantities over 5,000 units per SKU per order

## 3. Delivery Terms

- **Standard lead time:** 14 calendar days from confirmed purchase order
- **Delivery location:** FOB destination to Buyer's designated pet distribution warehouse
- **Delivery window:** ±2 business days from confirmed delivery date

## 4. Performance Standards

### 4.1 On-Time Delivery

- Target on-time delivery rate: **90% or higher** per rolling quarter
- Measurement: Shipment arriving within the confirmed delivery window

### 4.2 Delivery Penalties

- Shipments exceeding **7 calendar days** past the confirmed delivery date incur a penalty of **2% of the purchase order value per day**, capped at **20% of order value**.
- Penalties are applied as credits against future invoices.
- The Buyer must provide written notice of penalty assessment within 15 business days of delivery.

### 4.3 Chronic Non-Performance

- If the Supplier's on-time delivery rate falls below **50%** over any **rolling 90-day period**, the Buyer may:
  1. Issue a formal performance improvement notice
  2. Require a written corrective action plan within **10 business days**
  3. Escalate to **VP Pet Products** review within **5 business days** of the 90-day threshold breach
  4. Initiate qualification of alternate pet toy suppliers under Section 8

## 5. Quality and Safety Standards

- Incoming inspection acceptance rate: **98%** minimum
- All pet toys must comply with ASTM F963 (toy safety) and CPSIA (Consumer Product Safety)
- No toxic materials, choking hazards, or sharp edges permitted
- Defective units shall be replaced at Supplier's expense within 10 business days
- Three consecutive failed batch inspections trigger quality escalation per Section 4.3

## 6. Product Recall Cooperation

- Supplier must immediately notify Buyer of any product safety concerns
- Supplier bears full cost of voluntary or mandatory recalls due to manufacturing defects
- Supplier must maintain product liability insurance of minimum $5M per occurrence

## 7. Termination

- Either party may terminate with **90-day written notice**
- Immediate termination permitted for material breach unremedied within 30 days
- Chronic non-performance under Section 4.3 constitutes grounds for immediate termination
- Product safety violation constitutes grounds for immediate termination

## 8. Alternate Supplier Rights

- Buyer retains the right to qualify and activate alternate pet product suppliers at any time
- Upon triggering Section 4.3, Buyer may dual-source without reducing minimum volume commitment for 180 days
