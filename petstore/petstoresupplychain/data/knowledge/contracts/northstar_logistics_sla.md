# TailWag Logistics – Service Level Agreement

**Parties:** PetStore Retail Inc. ("Customer") and TailWag Logistics LLC ("Provider")
**Effective Date:** March 1, 2024
**Term:** 2 years

---

## 1. Services Covered

TailWag Logistics provides:
- Regional pet product freight and distribution services for the Northeast corridor
- Temperature-controlled shipping for pet food and health products
- Expedited same-day and next-day delivery options
- Warehouse-to-retail-partner distribution transfers
- White-glove delivery for fragile items (aquariums, cat trees)

## 2. Service Levels

### 2.1 Standard Delivery
- **Transit time:** 3–5 business days within Northeast region
- **On-time guarantee:** 95% of shipments delivered within quoted window

### 2.2 Expedited Delivery
- **Same-day:** Available for orders placed before 10:00 AM EST
- **Next-day:** Available for orders placed before 4:00 PM EST
- **Expedited surcharge:** 2.5x standard shipping rate
- **Air freight option:** 3x standard rate for critical shipments over 500 lbs

### 2.3 Temperature-Controlled Service
- Maintained between 35–75°F throughout transit
- Real-time temperature monitoring with alert notifications
- Guaranteed for all pet food and medication shipments
- Additional surcharge: 1.5x standard rate

### 2.4 Tracking and Visibility
- Real-time shipment tracking via API integration
- Proactive delay notifications within 2 hours of identified delay
- Retail partner delivery confirmation with photo proof

## 3. SLA Credits

| Metric | Target | Threshold for Credit | Credit Amount |
|---|---|---|---|
| On-Time Delivery | 95% | Below 90% in any month | 5% of monthly invoice |
| Temperature Compliance | 99.5% | Below 98% | Replacement cost + 15% |
| Damage Rate | <0.5% | Above 1.0% | Replacement cost + 10% |
| Tracking Availability | 99.9% | Below 99% | 2% of monthly invoice |

## 4. Escalation Path

1. **Level 1:** Pet Account Manager — response within 4 hours
2. **Level 2:** Regional Operations Director — response within 8 hours
3. **Level 3:** VP Logistics — response within 24 hours

## 5. Emergency Services

- TailWag maintains emergency capacity reserves for critical pet product shipments
- Customer may invoke emergency priority for retail partner stockout situations with Director-level approval
- Emergency routing available 24/7 including weekends and holidays
- Priority handling for pet food and medication emergencies (no surcharge for genuine stockout events)
