# BarkWood Crafts Ltd – Terms and Conditions of Supply

**Parties:** PetStore Retail Inc. ("Buyer") and BarkWood Crafts Ltd ("Supplier")
**Effective Date:** June 1, 2024
**Term:** 1 year, auto-renewing

---

## 1. Products

BarkWood Crafts supplies:
- Orthopedic Memory Foam Dog Beds (PRD-205)
- Cat Tree Towers (PRD-206)
- Custom pet furniture per PetStore design specifications

## 2. Pricing

- **Pricing model:** Fixed catalog pricing with quarterly adjustment window
- **Payment terms:** Net 45
- **Minimum order value:** $3,000 per purchase order
- **Seasonal volume discount:** 10% discount on orders over $20,000 (Q4 holiday season)

## 3. Delivery

- **Standard lead time:** 18 calendar days
- **Delivery terms:** FOB shipping point
- **Late delivery:** Supplier will use commercially reasonable efforts to meet delivery dates; formal penalty clause not included in base terms
- **Oversized items (cat trees):** Palletized freight required; no parcel shipping

## 4. Quality

- ISO 9001:2015 certified facility
- Certificate of conformance provided with each shipment
- All pet furniture must pass BIFMA (Business and Institutional Furniture Manufacturers Association) weight testing
- Fabric and foam materials must be non-toxic and pet-safe (OEKO-TEX Standard 100)
- Buyer may conduct on-site quality audits with 15-day notice

## 5. Alternate Sourcing

- BarkWood Crafts is a **pre-qualified alternate supplier** for pet toys and furniture per Buyer's Approved Vendor List
- BarkWood may be activated as secondary source within **72 hours** with Quality Engineering sign-off
- Emergency qualification process: Director approval + QE validation of last 3 lot inspection records

## 6. Warranty

- 18-month warranty from date of delivery
- Defective products replaced or credited at Buyer's option
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects; normal pet wear-and-tear excluded
